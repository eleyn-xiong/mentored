Szechuan Spicy Wontons

Description

A cult favorite at Dumpling Dynasty. These shrimp and pork wontons are tossed in a signature house-made chili oil, black vinegar, and garlic sauce. Itâ€™s a perfect balance of "Mala" (numbing heat) and savory sweetness.

Ingredients

  • Wonton wrappers (square)
  • Minced shrimp and ground pork
  • Roasted chili flakes and Sichuan peppercorns
  • Chinkiang black vinegar
  • Toasted sesame seeds and cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. The Sauce: Whisk together chili oil, black vinegar, soy sauce, and minced garlic in a bowl.
  2. The Filling: Mix shrimp and pork with a dash of white pepper and sesame oil.
  3. The Fold: Place a teaspoon of filling in the center, fold into a triangle, and bring the two corners together.
  4. The Finish: Boil wontons for 3-4 minutes until they float. Drain and toss immediately in the spicy sauce.
