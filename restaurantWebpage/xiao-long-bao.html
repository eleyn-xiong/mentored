Hand-crafted Xiao Long Bao

Description

Experience the golden ratio of filling to soup inspired by the Shandong tradition.

  • 2 cups All-purpose flour (high gluten preferred)
  • 1lb Ground pork shoulder
  • 200g Pork aspic (concentrated broth gelatin)
  • Fresh ginger and Shaoxing wine

Instructions

  1. The Dough: Knead flour and warm water until smooth; rest for 30 minutes.
  2. The Filling: Combine minced pork with finely chopped aspic cubes and seasonings
  3. The Fold: Roll wrappers into thin circles. Place filling in center and pleat tightly.
  4. The Steam: Line a bamboo steamer with cabbage leaves and steam for 8 minutes.
